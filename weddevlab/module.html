Module / Society Information

About the Society

Our school societies are student-led communities focused on leadership, creativity, and collaboration. From cultural to technical domains, these societies encourage holistic growth, allowing students to explore passions beyond academics.

Goals & Objectives

- Foster leadership and teamwork among students.
- Promote co-curricular engagement and participation.
- Develop creative and critical thinking skills through real-world problem-solving.
- Encourage innovation, communication, and responsibility.

Current Modules

1. Science & Innovation Club: For young inventors and curious minds.
2. Arts & Culture Society: Explore drama, dance, literature, and painting.
3. Eco Club: Focusing on sustainability and nature-based learning.
4. Tech Forum: Robotics, coding, app development, and tech competitions.
5. Language Circle: Emphasizes public speaking, debate, and languages.

Upcoming Events

- Innovation Fair 2025: Showcasing student science projects and inventions.
- Inter-School Tech Fest: A competition across coding, gaming, and web design.
- Annual Cultural Day: A celebration of art, music, drama, and traditions.
- Environment Awareness Week: Plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and debates.
